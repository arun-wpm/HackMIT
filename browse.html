Welcome to CrowdLec!

Our team is striving to make learning accessible for all. And for all, this includes students speaking other languages, students that are hard of hearing, etc. We are a crowd-sourced platform, where students can help edit subtitles of lectures, put in comments, questions, answers and notes.
